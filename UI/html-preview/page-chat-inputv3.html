Why not just sort by timeline?
Because teachers often jump back to previous slides or leave the PPT entirely. Timeline order doesn't equal slide order — semantic similarity is far more reliable.
What does K=3 debouncing mean?
It means three consecutive ASR chunks must point to the same slide before we actually switch pages — preventing one-off similarity spikes from causing jittery page jumps.
